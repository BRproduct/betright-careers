About the Role

This isn't a passenger seat. As Market Making 2IC at Bet Right, you'll be the engine behind our market making operation. You're the person the Head of Market Making trusts to run the floor, call the shots and make the tough risk decisions when they matter most.

You'll own the financial and operational performance of our sports market making function day-in, day-out. You'll build and mentor a crew of Market Makers, set the standard for market quality, and help forge the culture that makes Bet Right's markets the sharpest in the game.

Primary Function
What you'll own.
  • Step up as Head of Market Making when needed, with full accountability and no hesitation
  • Drive wagering strategy and market direction across the Bet Right group
  • Build a high-performance team of Market Makers who live and breathe the Bet Right mission
  • Coach, mentor and develop Market Makers and wagering graduates into the best version of themselves
  • Take hands-on ownership of risk across racing and sport, including real-time client-level decisions
  • Own and strengthen relationships with third-party data providers and regulatory bodies
  • Keep governance tight, with compliance, policies and procedures upheld across every corner of the floor
  • Step in on client advocacy and admin when it counts, because service standards never slip
Key Responsibilities
What a great day looks like.
  • Lock down the integrity and accuracy of all sports and racing market making systems: controls, staffing, financials
  • Dissect the previous day's results, hunt down anomalies and suspicious activity, and act fast
  • Build sharp, commercially competitive markets across all sports and benchmark them against the best
  • Keep the client watch list and Risk Management System spotless, every profile current, every condition noted
  • Make decisive, real-time bet confirmation calls using the RMS, CRM and Bet Right's information systems
  • Lead your Market Makers with purpose: clear KPIs, timely reviews, high standards
  • Keep the Head of Market Making and COO across every material event the moment it matters
  • Deliver the weekly market making report to management. Sharp, accurate, on time.
  • Zero errors in resulting. Every time. No exceptions.
  • Handle client complaints with professionalism and resolve them per the complaints procedure
  • Get markets live at least one day before events, without fail
